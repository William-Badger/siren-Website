BLOODBORNE

BloodBorne is a very popular videogame made in 2015 by the company FromSoftware. The game is a Souls-Like, which means it follows similar mechanics to the popular Dark Souls series by the same creators.



FromSoftware

FromSoftware is a Japanese company headed by the very popular Hidetake Miyazaki. They have a long list of popular titles, but their most popular is the Dark Souls series and BloodBorne, likely due to their awe inspiring level design and brutal difficulty.
