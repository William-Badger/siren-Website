BLOODBORNE

BloodBorne is a very popular videogame made in 2015 by the company FromSoftware. The game is a Souls-Like, which means it follows similar mechanics to the popular Dark Souls series by the same creators.



FromSoftware

FromSoftware is a Japanese company headed by the very popular Hidetake Miyazaki. They have a long list of popular titles, but their most popular is the Dark Souls series and BloodBorne. Their popularity likely due to their stunning level design and brutal difficulty.

The Setting

The game takes place in a Gothic/Victorian styled city called Yharnam, filled with beast-like enemies and bosses eluding to werewolves and vampires. About halfway through the game, the world expands into creepy forests, rocky cliffsides and a giant castle all filled with increasingly eldritch-like horrors.

The Story

You, the player, are being treated for an ailment called Paleblood through a blood transfusion by an old man named Gehrman. This treatment causes the player to go on a kind of spirit quest to rid a world of terrible beasts and cosmic entities. Completing the quest and returning to Gehrman grants you two options, to be dispatched by Gehrman and wake up in the real world healed or fight Gehrman. Upon beating Gehrman, the player essentially takes his places, guiding others through their treatment.

The Game Mechanics

The game is a 3D fighting game, utilizing dodging and different attack types to take down your great foes who are most often giant and tower over you. But each have weaknesses you can exploit by using different weapons and applying different effects to said weapons, such as fire or lightning.

The Trick Weapons

There are many weapons in this game, each with their own unique look and attack animation. They are called trick weapons because they can transform into a different version of itself, like a cane sword than can become a whip or a sword that can turn into a giant hammer.
